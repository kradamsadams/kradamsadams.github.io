REMIX

The object of this assignment was to combine various images and works of art to create one new image that had a new meaning. For my project I selected various images of animals which are either extinct or are endangered species. I used Adobe Photoshop to arrange a composite telling the story of these animals and their inevitable fate as they walked down a man-made path of destruction toward inevitable obliteration.
